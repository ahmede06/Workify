About Workify

Workify is a simple web application to help you manage your eco-footprint.

Why Choose Workify?

Image by Canva, 2025

Staying organized is crucial in today's fast-paced world. We offer streamline approaches to eco-sustainability, making it easier to keep track of the waste we throw away.

Key Features

  • Task-management: Figure out the course of your day with ease!
  • Calendar: Record trash days, gas-free days, or perhaps notable environmental events in Markham!
  • Notes: Keep track of your waste in a week in order to effectively track and manage your footprint!
  • Reminders: Remind yourself of key, eco-friendly info, such as what goes in the recycling!
